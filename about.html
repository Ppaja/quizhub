About

What is QuizHub?

QuizHub" is a web-based project that focuses on creating an interactive platform for hosting and playing quizzes.

The project aims to provide users with a fun and engaging experience while testing their knowledge through a variety of quiz topics and themes. Users can select different themes to customize their experience, and the project is designed to be a collaborative effort where contributors from around the world can work together to enhance the platform.

Everyone can contribute

QuizHub is open source and anyone can contribute to the project. Contributions are welcomed and appreciated!
